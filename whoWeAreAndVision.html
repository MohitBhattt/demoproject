About Us

Motivational Institute for Training & Reinforcement (MITR), agroup of enthusiastic people is working in this region with the idea of sustainable development of local communities ensuring the optimum utilization of resources in a scientific of resources in a scientific manner and with the involvement of people in every such endeavor.Since its inception MITR has made a sincere and remarkable attention in the development of Health, Education, Agriculture, Horticulture and overall social development. The development of Community Based Organization (CBOs) like SHGs, FIGs, Farmer& Federation has been the key approach for livelihood enhancement of the local communities

OUR MISSION

  • Providing opportunities to the individuals or groups representing different sectors of the community for self as well as social development through proper counseling and awareness after analyzing their needs.
  • Formulating groups of different people having knowledge about the locally available resources in the command area of the organization.
  • Motivating community members to identify their problems and needs at their own and also to suggest the possible measures to overcome such problems.
  • Facilitating the community members in developing their skills for solving the identified problems & further fulfilling such needs through possible efforts by the organization.
  • Making people aware about the indigenous & eco-friendly technologies in different development sectors like agriculture, horticulture etc.
  • Sensitizing people for optimum utilizations of natural resources.

OUR VISION

  • Leading the communities specifically the vulnerable groups and women folk towards prosperity in a sustainable manner.
  • To motivate the individuals and groups of people to take positive as well as constrictive initiatives for their sustainable livelihood development.
  • To build the capacity of rural communities, farmers, women, and students for self development.
  • To reinforce the efforts already been done or in progress through other development partners and also to support such endeavors.

25 Years Of Experience

With years of dedicated experience, Motivational Institute for Training & Reinforcement (MITR) has consistently worked towards the sustainable development of local communities.y fostering initiatives in health, education, agriculture, horticulture, and overall social growth.

Good Will Volunteers

With extensive experience in community service, Good Will Volunteers have been tirelessly working to uplift and empower underprivileged communities. Their efforts span across health, education, and livelihood enhancement, focusing on sustainable growth and resource optimization.

Most Trusted MITR

With years of unwavering dedication, MITR has earned its reputation as the most trusted partner in sustainable community development. Through impactful initiatives in health, education, agriculture, and social growth, MITR has empowered countless lives.
