Welcome to Motivation Institute for Training & Reinforcement(MITR)

Motivational Institute for Training & Reinforcement (MITR), a group of enthusiastic people is working in this region with the idea of sustainable development of local communities ensuring the optimum utilization of resources in a scientific of resources in a scientific manner and with the involvement of people in every such endeavor.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower vulnerable groups, especially women, by building their capacities and guiding them toward sustainable livelihoods. We aim to support and enhance development efforts for lasting and inclusive progress.
